What supplements should I be taking?

Supplements are an optional but extremely helpful addition to a weighlifting program. This page will share the ones I have used and have seen great results from. Disclaimer: do your own research before using any of these products, a link to a credible source detailing the specifics of the mentioned supplements is included below!

Supplements I recommend

Caffeine

Helped to increase the intensity of my workouts and made it easier to get to the gym on days I was tired

Whey protein

Made getting the necessary daily protein to sustain muscle sythesis much easier, especially as a college kid with limited time to cook

Creatine

Well studied, naturally occurring supplement which optimizies muscle energy production and made it easier to put on size and strength
